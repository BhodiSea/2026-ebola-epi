Cholera — Democratic Republic of the Congo — 1 September 2024

As of 1 September 2024, the Democratic Republic of the Congo has reported 35,278 suspected cholera cases, including 6,842 confirmed and 614 deaths (case fatality ratio 1.74%) since the beginning of 2024. The outbreak is ongoing across 22 health zones in eight provinces, with North Kivu, Ituri, and South Kivu reporting the highest burden. Oral cholera vaccination campaigns have reached 1.2 million people in priority zones.
