Mpox (clade I) — Democratic Republic of the Congo — 31 December 2023

As of 31 December 2023, the Democratic Republic of the Congo has reported 14,626 suspected mpox cases, including 1,002 confirmed and 654 deaths (case fatality ratio 4.5%) during 2023. The clade I outbreak continues to expand across multiple provinces, with Équateur, Sud-Kivu, and Sankuru recording the highest numbers of suspected cases. This represents a 7% increase in suspected cases compared to the same period in 2022. Of confirmed cases, a notable proportion (approximately 30%) involve children under 15 years of age.
