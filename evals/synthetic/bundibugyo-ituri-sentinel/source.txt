Bundibugyo virus disease outbreak — Democratic Republic of Congo — situation report 12 May 2026

As of 12 May 2026, a total of 61 confirmed cases and 18 deaths (case-fatality ratio 29.5%) have been reported in Ituri Province, Democratic Republic of Congo since the outbreak was declared on 28 March 2026. The pathogen has been confirmed as Bundibugyo ebolavirus (species Zaire ebolavirus, clade Bundibugyo) by Institut National de Recherche Biomédicale. Response operations are ongoing in Bunia and Irumu health zones.
