Ebola virus disease (Sudan strain) — Uganda — 30 November 2022

As of 30 November 2022, Uganda reports 141 total cases (87 confirmed, 54 probable) and 55 deaths (case fatality ratio 39.0%) since the outbreak began on 20 September 2022. The outbreak primarily affects Mubende and Kassanda districts. A total of 6 healthcare workers have been infected (4 confirmed, 2 probable), with 3 healthcare worker deaths. Ring vaccination using the Sabin MV-ZEBOV-GP candidate vaccine is ongoing. Contact tracing has identified 4,247 contacts, of whom 1,382 are under active follow-up.
