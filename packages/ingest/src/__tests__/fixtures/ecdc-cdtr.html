Communicable Disease Threats Report, Week 22, 2026

Published: 30 May 2026

This issue of the ECDC Communicable Disease Threats Report covers threats to public health from communicable diseases in the European Union and European Economic Area.

Bundibugyo virus disease — Democratic Republic of the Congo

As of 27 May 2026, the Democratic Republic of the Congo has reported a total of 108 confirmed cases of Bundibugyo virus disease in Ituri Province, including 23 deaths (case fatality rate 21.3%). The outbreak is ongoing with active transmission in three health zones.

ECDC risk assessment for EU/EEA: LOW. Imported cases to the EU remain unlikely given the remote location of affected health zones and limited international travel.
