Marburg virus disease — United Republic of Tanzania — 1 May 2026

As of 1 May 2026, the United Republic of Tanzania reports 5 confirmed cases and 2 deaths (case fatality ratio 40.0%) of Marburg virus disease. Cases are concentrated in Arusha Region. The index case was identified on 22 April 2026 following investigation of a cluster of unexplained febrile deaths at Arusha Regional Referral Hospital. Laboratory confirmation was obtained by the National Public Health Laboratory in Dar es Salaam and verified by the Institut Pasteur de Dakar. Contact tracing has identified 73 contacts currently under 21-day health monitoring. No cases have been detected outside Arusha Region.
